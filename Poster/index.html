POLLUTION

Pollution is the introduction of harmful materials into the environment.
Pollutants can be natural, such as volcanic ash. They can also be created by
human activity, such as trash or runoff produced by factories. Pollutants damage
the quality of air, water, and land.

The different types of pollution include:

- Air pollution
- Water pollution.
- Soil pollution.
- Radioactive pollution.
- Noise pollution.
